Bacon-wrapped Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Jalapeno Poppers are delicious. How much more amazing would they be if you wrapped them in bacon?!

This recipe is a great twist on a delicious classic.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 12 jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise, seeds and membranes removed
  • 12 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.

  2. Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended. Fill each jalape√±o half with cheese mixture. Put halves back together and wrap each stuffed pepper with a slice of bacon. Arrange bacon-wrapped peppers on the prepared baking sheet.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy, 25 to 35 minutes.
