Best Cheese Dip

Description

What more could you want? Creamy, cheesey, dippy... dip.

This warm recipe is great for a cool day, or any day really.

Ingredients

  • Meat: ground beef or ground pork sausage
  • Diced onion
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chile pepper
  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup

Steps

  1. Cook the ground meat and onion in a skillet. Transfer the meat to a slow cooker.

  2. Layer the cheese cubes on the meat mixture.

  3. Blend the canned tomatoes until smooth, then pour over the cheese with the soup.

  4. Cook on High until the cheese is completely melted.
